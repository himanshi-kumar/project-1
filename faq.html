Frequently Asked Questions

Your guide to use the Mausam App

1. How accurate is the weather forecast?

Weather forecasts are based on data from meteorological agencies and advanced models, but accuracy can vary depending on location and weather conditions. Short-term forecasts (1-2 days) tend to be more accurate than long-term predictions.

2. Is the app available for both iOS and Android?

Yes! Mausam can be used in both iOS and Android.

3. Can I set location preferences?

Absolutely! You can set your preferred locations to get personalized weather updates.

4. How often is the weather data updated?

Weather data is updated every hour to provide you with the most current information.

5. Do I need an internet connection to use the app?

Yes, an internet connection is required to access real-time weather data and forecasts.

6. How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support via the "Contact Us" section in the app or email us at support@mausam.com.

7. Does the app provide hourly or daily forecasts?

Yes, the app currently provides daily forecasts.
